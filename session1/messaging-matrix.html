Club Messaging Matrix

Create a branding plan to make your club irresistible to members and guests

Club Messaging Matrix (1:10–1:25)

Introduction

What makes your club stand out? Is it your vibrant community, professional focus, or fun meetings? Your club's brand is its heartbeat, and as officers, you shape it. Let's create a Club Messaging Matrix to make your club irresistible to members and guests.

Purpose

I'll guide you through building a simple matrix to define your club's identity and outreach plan, ensuring your messages shine on X, flyers, and beyond.

Supporting Points

1. Define Your Core Message
Answer: Who are we? Who's our audience? For example, my club's message is "Empowering professionals to speak with confidence." We target young workers via LinkedIn. A 2024 survey showed clubs with clear brands attract 25% more guests.

2. Choose Your Channels
Pick 2–3 platforms: X for quick updates, flyers for local events, or email for member updates. Our club's X post about a speech contest got 30 retweets and 5 guests. Start small—track likes or RSVPs as wins.

3. Craft Consistent Messages
Use a matrix: Core message, channel, frequency. Example: "Post 'Join our speech contest!' on X weekly." Consistency builds recognition. In District 106, a club's consistent branding doubled guest turnout in six months.

Conclusion and Call to Action

Your club's brand is its voice. Build a Messaging Matrix today to attract and retain members. Your challenge: Draft your matrix now, and bring a PR calendar to our August training. Let's make your club the talk of the town!
