Welcome Speech

Introduction
Imagine a Toastmasters club where guests flock to every meeting, inspired by a vibrant online presence and a clear brand. That's the power of a great VPPR. As District Director, I've seen clubs like yours in District 106 skyrocket from a few guests to President's Distinguished, thanks to stellar PR. Welcome to our Club Officer Training for July 17, 2025!

Purpose
Today, we'll equip you, especially our VPPRs, with tools to lead with confidence, align your team, and build a magnetic club identity. I'll outline our objectives and how this training helps your club earn DCP credit.

Supporting Points

1. Why VPPRs Are Game-Changers
The Club Leadership Handbook calls VPPRs the voice of the club. Your social media posts, flyers, and events bring in guests. Last year, a VPPR's X campaign brought 10 new members to a struggling club. You'll learn how to do this today.

2. Training Objectives
We'll dive into retention, share PR success stories, and focus on VPPR strategies to boost your club's brand. You'll leave with a plan to make your club the talk of the town.

3. Earning DCP Credit
Attend at least 2 hours of training, including one peer session, to earn DCP credit. Today's 90-minute session, plus an elective in July or August, gets you there. Use Club Central to track progress.

Conclusion and Call to Action
VPPRs, you're the megaphone for your club's success. Dive into today's sessions, especially our VPPR breakout, and connect with peers. Grab your Club Leadership Handbook and let's make your club shine!
