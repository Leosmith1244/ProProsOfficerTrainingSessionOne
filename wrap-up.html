Wrap-Up & Homework (1:25–1:30)

Introduction

Wow, VPPRs, you've stepped up today! You've learned to boost retention, craft a killer brand, and own your role. Your club's future is bright because of you.

Purpose

Let's recap and set you up with homework to amplify your club's voice by August.

Supporting Points

1. Retention Starts with PR
Your branding creates a welcoming vibe that keeps members. Post that member story or host an event.

2. Branding is Your Superpower
Your Messaging Matrix will draw guests. Use X or flyers to spread the word.

3. VPPRs Lead the Way
Use the Brand Manual and Club Central to execute your role. Connect with mentors for support.

Conclusion and Call to Action

VPPRs, you're the voice of your club. For August, bring a PR calendar (e.g., weekly X posts) and club goals (e.g., 4 new members). Network today, and let's make 2025–2026 Distinguished!

Takeaways

  • Post member stories to boost retention.
  • Use the Messaging Matrix for consistent branding.
  • Track progress in Club Central.

Activity: Leadership Pledge

VPPRs share one word for their PR goal (e.g., "Engage") at the front. Commit to leading your club to greatness!
