Panel discussion on cross-role collaboration strategies

Panel: Club Officer Chemistry (0:10–0:30)

Moderator Introduction

Great clubs thrive when officers work as a team. Our panel of experienced leaders will share how different roles can collaborate for maximum impact.

Panelist 1: President & VPE Partnership

"Our President and VPE meet weekly to align educational goals with club vision. We use a shared Google Sheet to track Pathways progress and plan mentoring."

Panelist 2: VPM & VPPR Synergy

"We coordinate guest follow-ups - the VPPR sends branded thank-you emails while I handle membership questions. This doubled our guest-to-member conversion."

Panelist 3: Secretary-Treasurer Team

"We created a finance dashboard showing dues status, which helps the Secretary remind members and the Treasurer forecast budgets."

Collaboration Strategies

  • Monthly officer "huddles" (30 min virtual meetings)
  • Shared digital workspace (Google Drive/Teams)
  • Role-specific check-ins (VPE+VPM for new member onboarding)

Activity: Role Connection Mapping

Officers draw lines between their roles showing collaboration points (e.g., VPPR←→VPM for guest follow-ups). Share most important connection with the group.
